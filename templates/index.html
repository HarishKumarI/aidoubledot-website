Solution Areas

Image and Video Insights

Build domain-specific pattern recognition, classification, image analysis, attribute extraction, and image similarity models. We have built an end-to-end book digitisation solution with a custom multilingual OCR, designed computer vision solutions to analyse aesthetics of fashion products, developed a solution for vision-based check-out technology for a vending machine.

Multilingual Natural Language Processing

Build a conversational intelligence, analysis of customer reviews and feedback, multilingual natural language text generation and semantic processing of documents and unstructured content. For example, our team has helped implement extracting aspect-sentiment pairs, understanding of customer queries and recommend resolution, and multilingual predictive input technologies.

Analytics and Prediction Models

Build data science solutions for customer insights, recommendation systems, forecasting, and prediction models. With our lab's strength in processing image, text and tabular data, our prediction models can construct signals from any form of data. Our experts have worked on detecting customer churn from time series data, forecasted loan recovery from historical data, built ranking and recommendation models to spot trending products from proxy signals.

How it Works

Solutioning duration could be from 3 months to 12 months depending on the complexity of the problem. The solution development process can viewed as three stages, with revisits throughout the lifecycle, as we come across more insights, data or real-world challenges.

1
Problem Analysis & Preparation

We interact with your business team and study the problem, data readiness and user scenarios, and come up with a directional approach. We prepare concrete plans to acquire & curate data and to validate key value, usability & feasibility risks of the candidate solutions. Based on your tech team's skill levels we build a training plan and get them started on it.

Discovery & Implementation

We design sprints to iterate over tens of ideas a week, following a continuous discovery and delivery process to see solution components in action sooner. Our lab will create or customise our codebase and technologies to power faster discovery and implementation. Where advanced R&D is required, our lab will carry them out and create results usable by your tech team.

2
3
Deployment & Refinement

We ensure the deployment of the solution meets the expected scale and well instrumented to collect real-world usage data to both monitor business impact and technical limitations. We help the team iteratively improve the solution by analysing usage collected and coming up with fixes to improve the quality. By the end of this your tech team will be equipped to manage the solution

About

AI Double Dot is founded by Ram Prakash H, who has a pedigree of creating several successful real-world machine learning based solutions over more than a decade in the areas covering multilingual NLP and Computer Vision. He created Quillpad a technology for Indian language input for which he holds a patent and was listed among the top 20 innovators by MIT TR35. He has been a serial entrepreneur and co-founded Tachyon Technologies, Cognirel Technologies and Stylumia Intelligence. He is also a Data Science and Machine Learning expert consultant for Flipkart.
