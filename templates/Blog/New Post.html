## Plugins</p>

<p>The killer feature of `markdown-it` is very effective support of<br />
[syntax plugins](https://www.npmjs.org/browse/keyword/markdown-it-plugin).</p>

<p><br />
### [Emojies](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-emoji)</p>

<p>&gt; Classic markup: :wink: :crush: :cry: :tear: :laughing: :yum:<br />
&gt;<br />
&gt; Shortcuts (emoticons): :-) :-( 8-) ;)</p>

<p>see [how to change output](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-emoji#change-output) with twemoji.</p>

<p><br />
### [Subscript](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-sub) / [Superscript](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-sup)</p>

<p>- 19^th^<br />
- H~2~O</p>

<p><br />
### [\&lt;ins&gt;](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-ins)</p>

<p>++Inserted text++</p>

<p><br />
### [\&lt;mark&gt;](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-mark)</p>

<p>==Marked text==</p>

<p><br />
### [Footnotes](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-footnote)</p>

<p>Footnote 1 link[^first].</p>

<p>Footnote 2 link[^second].</p>

<p>Inline footnote^[Text of inline footnote] definition.</p>

<p>Duplicated footnote reference[^second].</p>

<p>[^first]: Footnote **can have markup**</p>

<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; and multiple paragraphs.</p>

<p>[^second]: Footnote text.</p>

<p><br />
### [Definition lists](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-deflist)</p>

<p>Term 1</p>

<p>: &nbsp; Definition 1<br />
with lazy continuation.</p>

<p>Term 2 with *inline markup*</p>

<p>: &nbsp; Definition 2</p>

<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; { some code, part of Definition 2 }</p>

<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; Third paragraph of definition 2.</p>

<p>_Compact style:_</p>

<p>Term 1<br />
&nbsp; ~ Definition 1</p>

<p>Term 2<br />
&nbsp; ~ Definition 2a<br />
&nbsp; ~ Definition 2b</p>

<p><br />
### [Abbreviations](https://github.com/markdown-it/markdown-it-abbr)</p>

<p>This is HTML abbreviation example.</p>

<p>It converts &quot;HTML&quot;, but keep intact partial entries like &quot;xxxHTMLyyy&quot; and so on.</p>

<p>*[HTML]: Hyper Text Markup Language</p>
